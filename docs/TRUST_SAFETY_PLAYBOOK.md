# Trust & Safety playbook

Mirrored in-app at `/ops/playbook`.

1. **Never auto-clear on AI signals** — human TrustReview required  
2. **Evidence pack completeness** — matrix required items present  
3. **Security vertical** — SIA evidence; waitlist until ops ready  
4. **Care vertical** — DBS + safeguarding; not a CQC agency claim  
5. **Incident escalation** — HIGH/CRITICAL: fast ack, freeze if needed, audit  
6. **Client communication** — no overclaim of vetting depth  

## Decision types
- CLEAR — verified for category + tier  
- REQUEST_MORE — incomplete or inconsistent pack  
- WAITLIST — vertical not active  
- REJECT / SUSPEND — material risk or fraud signals
